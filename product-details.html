Marine Plywood

BWP (IS:710)

About
the product

Marine Plywood is manufactured using a quad press technology which produces a warp free, super strong bond that has zero surface core gap characteristics to the plywood.

The Marine Plywood undergoes three types of treatments during the manufacturing process. They are bonded with superior bonding quality of resin to resist plywood for Boiling Water Proof. The adhesive used to bind the layers are made at Afyun. The in-house adhesive is free of toxins making the product safe for the environment. Each layer is treated with a combination of various toxicants by VPT(vacuum pressure treatment). These methods give a super smooth surface to the ply and give it the ability to resist borers, which ensures quality and durability of the furniture and other builds.

Marine Plywood is widely used in kitchens, bathrooms and outdoors spaces where the chances of direct contact with water are high. The plywood is much more reliable than the MR or BWR Plywoods as it can withstand all kinds of wear and tear. These plywoods are suitable for living room furniture and wardrobes, oftentimes the Marine Plywoods are also used in ships and boats.
