Our Vision

Our vision is to rise above the flaws and bring new possibilities in the plywood industry.

We continue to achieve this through proper planning and execution at every instance. The brand Afyun stands as the symbol of quality, drawing from an outcome of strength and durability. Our journey hasn’t ended yet. We gear up to cross greater milestones and climb many stairs of success ahead.

Our Mission

We know that producing the best quality goods comes with healthy and satisfied employees.

That is why we prioritise the well being of our employees just as much as the quality of our products. Our well maintained machinery and a safe working environment plays a major role in making the finest plywoods in the industry.
